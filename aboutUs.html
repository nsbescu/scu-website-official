About Us

Our NSBE chapter is the home of Black engineering talent at Santa Clara University. With 50+ members of varying STEM disciplines on campus, we participate in campus diversity efforts, volunteer in our local community, and are committed to fulfilling the NSBE mission statement. Since its founding in 2015, our chapter has engaged in pre-collegiate education initiatives, impressed corporate sponsors across the Bay Area, and trained aspiring professionals ready to positively impact whatever community they come across.

Mission Statement

"to increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community." - National Society of Black Engineers
